The Start Line Crew

Start Line Crew The start line crew are tasked with starting a race and ending the race. They do this by forming the bikes on the grid according to their qualifying position. Once the grid is formed race control will then inform the crew to release the bikes on their warm up lap. Once the bikes have returned from their warm up lap and and reformed onto the grid, race control will then say 'Start line you are ready for a race start' at which point the flag man at the front of the grid points at the race start lights and scampers of the track. A member of the start line in charge of the lights then will put red lights out from 1 to 5 seconds... no longer.
