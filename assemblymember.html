The Assembly Crew

Assembly Crew The assembly crew are another type of marshal. There job is hold the riders in the assembly area, until race control release them on circuit for race or practice session. If the riders are going out for a race they will be assigned of spot on a 'dummy grid' this reflects their position on the starting grid. If they are going out for a practice session, they will hand over a practice pass, if they don't have a practice pass they are not allowed on to the track.
