The Start Line Crew

Technical Crew This team are in charge of making sure that the riders bikes and riding equipment are within regulations. On the night before racing begins, Technical will scrutineer every competitors bike and riding equipment. They will be looking for any dangerous faults on the bike, such as loose bolts. They will also check to see that various points on the bike are lock wired for example the oil filler cap, brake caliper bolts... Technical will also check a riders crash helmet, leathers, gloves and boots for faults. It is also a requirement that a rider has an I.D. tag showing their name, blood type and any allergies and if they are on any medication.
