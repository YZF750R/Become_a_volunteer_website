Race Control

Race is the place where everything that happens on track and in the paddock is co-ordinated. The person in charge of the event is called the Race Director, there will also be a Clerk of the Course, Race Secretary. All these titles a throw Back from when the first motorsport events at the start of the 20th century and the governing bodies decided to adopt the same organizational structure as horse racing. The Race Director and Clerk of the Course can be the same person, they must be licenced by the A.C.U. (Auto Cycle Union) to run an event. They will be in charge of of when a race starts, when it ends and any disciplinary actions that need to be dealt with, for example if a rider has overtaken under waved yellows.
