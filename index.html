Become a volunteer in for motorcyle motorsport

If you’ve always wanted to be a part of the exciting Road Racing scene, but aren’t able to actually compete, you are not alone. There are many reasons for this ranging from finance to family commitments, but you can join in and be a vital part of the "A-Team" that makes it all happen. Without marshals, technical officials, recovery crews, assembly area staff and start line officials, meetings simply can’t happen. All of these posts are vital to the efficient, safe and friendly running of our events. To begin with, everyone starts as a trainee and is accompanied by experienced and friendly regulars. Training sessions are available, ranging from incident reporting, flag marshalling, handling damaged machines, fire fighting, dealing with injured riders and liaison with the medical staff and Race Control.
